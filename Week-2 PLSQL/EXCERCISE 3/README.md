# Exercise 3: Stored Procedures

## Scenario 1: ProcessMonthlyInterest

### Objective

Apply monthly interest to all savings accounts.

### Logic

* Identify savings accounts.
* Calculate 1% interest on the current balance.
* Update account balances.

### Result

The procedure successfully applied monthly interest to all savings accounts.

---

## Scenario 2: UpdateEmployeeBonus

### Objective

Provide a bonus to employees in a specified department.

### Logic

* Accept department name and bonus percentage as parameters.
* Increase employee salaries based on the bonus percentage.

### Result

The procedure successfully updated employee salaries for the specified department.

---

## Scenario 3: TransferFunds

### Objective

Transfer money between two accounts.

### Logic

* Check the source account balance.
* Verify sufficient funds are available.
* Deduct the amount from the source account.
* Add the amount to the destination account.

### Result

The procedure successfully transferred funds between accounts while validating account balance.

---

## Conclusion

This exercise demonstrates the creation and execution of stored procedures for banking operations such as interest calculation, employee bonus processing, and fund transfers.
